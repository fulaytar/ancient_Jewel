About the Game

  • Step back in time and uncover the mysteries of the ancient world with Ancient Jewel, a captivating match-3 puzzle game that takes you on a journey through history. In this game, you’ll match ancient jewels to solve puzzles and unlock the secrets of forgotten civilizations.

  • Each level presents unique challenges, from clearing specific jewels to navigating tricky obstacles, all set against a backdrop of beautifully rendered ancient temples and ruins.

  • As you progress, you’ll unlock powerful boosters and special abilities to help you conquer even the most difficult levels. With hundreds of levels to explore, daily rewards, and special events, Ancient Jewel offers endless entertainment for players of all ages.

  • Whether you’re a history buff or just love a good puzzle, this game is sure to captivate you from the very first level. Start your adventure today and see how many ancient secrets you can uncover!

    How to play

    • Swap adjacent jewels to create rows or columns of three or more matching ones

    • Match four or more jewels to create special jewels with unique effects

    • Remove obstacles like stone blocks, vines, and more by matching nearby jewels

    • Complete levels by collecting specific jewels or achieving score targets

    • Utilize special boosters to help clear challenging levels

    • Progress through the map by completing levels and unlocking new challenges

    • Join special events and challenges for additional rewards

    • Compare scores and compete with friends by connecting the game to social media
